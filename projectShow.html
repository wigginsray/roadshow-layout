    Stryker CMF

    NASM 2021

    CHALLENGE  

    CMF came to us in need of a virtual sales event that was high energy and designed to shake attendees of ‘zoom fatigue’. In response we created a one-of-a-kind, virtual venue and brought all their speakers to life with a mix of high-end technology, green screen and live show calling. 

    CLIENT  

    Stryker US 

    INDUSTRY 

    Medical

    Approach

    Our approach was simple: make the virtual event something people actually wanted to attend on their computer. To do this we focused on high production value, quality entertainment and digital user experience that the guests would actually enjoy instead of making it feel like and work obligation . In order to facilitate this, we created a custom site which also hosted the content post event. The virtual livestream featured a countdown to the live event, a custom ‘show opener’, company commercial breaks, wellness moments to reduce screen fatigue and a 1-hour hosted awards show. We provided 360degree services in order to bring an event of this size to life including:​ 


    • Overall Creative and Brand Strategy​ 
    • Virtual Event Program Planning​ 
    • Copywriting and Scripting​ 
    • Video Production and Editing​ 
    • 3D Design and Animation​ 
    • Wed Development and Hosting​ 
    • Live stream Webcasting and Live Show calling
